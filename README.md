## About Lyza's Rampage
A short, simple beat 'em up about a cat out for revenge! Lyza must hop on her robot vacuum and fight her way through the neighborhood to reach her nemesis, Beanie.

## Made with:
- Godot 4
- Aseprite
- Krita
- Ardour
